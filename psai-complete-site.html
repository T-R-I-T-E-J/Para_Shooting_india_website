Our Story
About STC Para
Shooting
The official para shooting body under the Paralympic Committee of India, recognised by the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports
Our Mission
Empowering Athletes,
Transforming Lives

STC Para Shooting (Paralympic Committee of India) was founded with the view to promote and popularize shooting sports among para-athletes in India. We are committed to developing world-class shooters.

Recognised by the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports, Government of India, and affiliated with IPC Germany, IWAS UK, and APC UAE — we operate at the highest levels of international para sport governance.

Through rigorous national championships, selection trials, classification camps and international exposure, we create pathways for para-athletes from every state in India to compete and excel on the world stage.

Eligibility Guide
Sport Classification
Understanding the classification system for para shooting — ensuring fair competition by grouping athletes with similar functional profiles
Categories
Shooting Classes
SH1
SH1
Shooting class SH1
Pistol & Rifle
Athletes who shoot with a pistol or rifle and have full arm function but may have lower limb impairments. Can hold the firearm without support.
P1 - 10M Air PistolP4 - 50M PistolR1 - 10M Air RifleR6 - 50M Rifle Prone
SH2
SH2
Shooting class SH2
Rifle only
Athletes with impaired arm function who need a shooting stand to support the firearm. Uses a support stand attached to the wheelchair or bench.
R3 - 10M Air Rifle ProneR5 - 10M Air Rifle StdR7 - 50M Rifle 3 Pos
VI
VI
Visually Impaired
B1, B2, B3
Athletes with visual impairments. Uses electronic sighting systems. B1 = totally blind, B2 = some light perception, B3 = limited visual acuity.
10M Air Rifle (VI)10M Air Pistol (VI)
SG
SG-L
Shotgun Lower Limb
Para Trap
Athletes with lower limb impairments who compete in shotgun disciplines. Includes Para Trap and Para Skeet events.
PT2 - Para Trap SG-LPT3 - Para Trap SG-S
SG
SG-S
Shotgun Seated
Para Trap/Skeet
Athletes who compete in shotgun events from a seated position due to impairments affecting both lower and upper body function.
Para Trap SeatedPara Skeet Seated
Junior
J
Junior Category
Under 21
Junior categories exist for athletes under 21 years. Denoted by "J" suffix — P1J, R1J etc. Separate rankings from senior events.
P1J - Junior PistolR1J - Junior RifleAll SH1/SH2 Junior
